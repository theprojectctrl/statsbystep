Our Mission

Empowering blue-collar, rural, and family-helper workers with the data and tools they need to grow their careers and businesses.

Founder's Message

Hi, I'm Amanda Ramirez, founder of Stats by Step. I created this platform to shine a light on the hard work and creativity of people who are often overlooked. By making it easy to track and showcase your achievements, I hope to help you unlock new opportunities and build a brighter future.

Impact & Vision

  • Translating hands-on labor into clear, credible data
  • Helping users build trust and attract more clients
  • Creating a supportive, diverse community
  • Building a sustainable, scalable platform for real-world impact
